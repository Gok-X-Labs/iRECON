# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a respectful and professional environment for all contributors.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Provide helpful feedback
- Focus on improving the project

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or abusive language
- Disruptive or unprofessional conduct
- Intentional misuse of the project

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove contributions or restrict access if guidelines are violated.

---

## Scope

This applies to all interactions within the project, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.
